Berry Galette

Description

In this recipe, we will be making two simple but delicious galettes.

Original recipe by joshuacooksthendraws.


Ingredients

  • 2 pie doughs
  • 1 pound strawberries
  • 1 carton blueberries
  • Sugar
  • Corn starch
  • Vanilla bean paste
  • Strawberry jam
  • Blueberry compote
  • Egg wash
  • Demerara sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Prepare your fillings.

    Strawberries: Slice 1 pound strawberries and add them to a bowl. I then add 2 tablespoons sugar, around 1.5 tablespoons corn starch, and 1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste. Mix thoroughly.

    Blueberries: Add 1 carton of blueberries into a bowl. Then add 2 tablespoons sugar and around 1.5 tablespoons corn starch. Mix thoroughly.

  3. Roll out two pie doughs. The shape is not so important. Place onto a baking sheet with parchment paper placed on top (in case of any leaks).

    Strawberries: Spread out around 1/4 cup strawberry jam onto the pie dough intended for the strawberries. Then place the strawberry mixture onto the pie dough. Leave ample space at the edge.

    Blueberries: Spread out around 1/4 cup blueberry compote onto the pie dough intended for the blueberries. Then place the blueberry mixture onto the pie dough. Leave ample space at the edge.

  4. Fold the pie dough over the fruit. Then brush the pie dough with egg wash and sprinkle demerara sugar on top.
  5. Place both galettes into a 400 degrees F oven and bake for 40-45 minutes until the filling is bubbling and the crust is golden brown.
  6. Let cool slightly and enjoy!
